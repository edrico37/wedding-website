FAQ

What about the ceremony?

We decided to keep things small and have a ceremony on February 11, 2017 in Doylestown, PA with just our immediate family.

What's the deal with the pajamas?

Every year we celebrate our anniversary with a "nothing weekend." We make no plans and wear our pajamas all day!
We're excited to share this tradition with all of you and hope you will enjoy it as much as we do.

So what's the dress code exactly?

Pajamas! Or whatever you wear when you're lounging around the house. Think onesies, flannel, sweatpants, and robes.

I'm on board! But I don't want to wear my pajamas outside. What should I do?

No problem! We'll have changing areas set up at the venue and a place to check your bag.

Is it alright if I bring my kids?

To allow everyone, including parents, an evening of relaxation we have chosen for our party to be an adult only occasion. We hope this advance notice means you are still able to join the celebration and will enjoy having the evening off!

I have a question that wasn't answered here. How should I contact you guys?

If you have any other questions, just send us an email at dylanandjaclyn@gmail.com.
