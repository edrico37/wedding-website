Getting There

By Public Transit

The venue is easily accessible by subway. It's just a few blocks from the R W 4 5 1 lines and is within walking distance of the 2 3 A C J Z lines. For schedules/maps, visit mta.info.

By Car

The venue is a quick cab ride from Penn Station. It's directly across the street from the famous Charging Bull sculpture. If you're driving yourself, a map of nearby parking garages can be found here.
